### Help102 is a web app built for the ENGR 102 course (intro to Python) taken by all incoming engineering freshmen at Texas A&M. The web app provides 3 main resources in the form of a learning page, a review page, and a labs page.
## Why build this?
### Since my high school years, I've been very passionate about teaching others, whether it be math or chess or computer science. I love seeing the joy others feel when the finally understand a concept or when they are able to build something cool.
